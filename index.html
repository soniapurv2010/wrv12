What's Included

Western Railways v12 is a massive simulation covering the entire Western Railway zone of India — from Kota to New Delhi, across Vadodara, Ahmedabad, Bhopal, Itarsi and beyond. Every section has been painstakingly detailed with real-world trackwork, signals, scenery, station layouts and sounds.

11 Sections Covered
  • KOTA Jn → New Delhi (Via Mathura)
  • KOTA Jn → VADODARA Jn
  • VADODARA Jn → AHMEDABAD Jn
  • AHMEDABAD Jn → MEHSANA Jn
  • AHMEDABAD Jn → RAJKOT Jn
  • AHMEDABAD Jn → BHUJ
  • VADODARA Jn → BHOPAL Jn (Via Ratlam, Nagda, Ujjain)
  • BHOPAL Jn → ITARSI Jn
  • ITARSI Jn → JABALPUR Jn
  • ITARSI Jn → NAGPUR Jn
  • UJJAIN Jn → INDORE BG
